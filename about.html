Building Smarter Observability

LogFoundry was founded to transform how teams interact with their log data, turning complexity into clarity with AI-powered insights.

Our Mission

Why we do what we do

At LogFoundry, we believe that every engineering team deserves world-class observability without the operational overhead. Traditional log management solutions require extensive setup, tuning, and maintenance - distracting teams from their core work.

We built LogFoundry on the Elastic Stack to give teams:

  • Pre-configured dashboards tailored to their stack
  • AI-powered anomaly detection out of the box
  • Managed infrastructure that scales with their needs
  • Actionable insights rather than raw data overload

Our mission is to help teams move faster by providing intelligent observability that just works.
