Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our onboarding process

How long does onboarding typically take? +

Most customers are up and running within 2-3 business days after submitting their information. Complex environments may take slightly longer, but we'll keep you informed throughout the process.

What information do you need to create my dashboard? +

We primarily need to know about your technology stack (backend frameworks, databases, etc.), the types of logs you generate, and which metrics are most important to your team. The more detail you can provide in the onboarding form, the better we can tailor your dashboard.

Can I modify the dashboard after setup? +

Absolutely! We provide you with full access to your Elastic cluster and Kibana instance, so you can customize dashboards, create new visualizations, and adjust alerts as needed. We also offer training sessions to help your team get the most out of the platform.

Is there a trial period? +

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial so you can evaluate LogFoundry with your actual data and workflows. There's no obligation to continue if it doesn't meet your needs.
