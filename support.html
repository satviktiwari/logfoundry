Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with LogFoundry?
Getting started with LogFoundry is easy. Sign up for an account, install our agent, and start collecting logs from your applications. Check out our documentation for detailed setup instructions.
What types of logs can I collect?
LogFoundry supports various log formats including application logs, system logs, security logs, and custom log formats. We also support structured logging formats like JSON.
How secure is my log data?
We take security seriously. All log data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We also offer role-based access control and audit logging to help you maintain compliance.
Can I integrate LogFoundry with other tools?
Yes, LogFoundry offers integrations with popular tools and platforms. Check our integrations page for a complete list of supported integrations.
